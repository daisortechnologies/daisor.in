✦ Flagship Product

SmartFill-100

Intelligent Water Level Controller

SmartFill-100 is a premium microcontroller-based water level controller designed to automate water pumping systems in homes, offices, and buildings. With advanced sensing technology and intelligent protection features, it ensures reliable tank filling, protects your pump, and eliminates the need for manual monitoring. SmartFill-100 automatically controls your water pump based on tank water level, ensuring the tank is filled efficiently without manual intervention.
